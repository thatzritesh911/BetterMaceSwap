Features
Auto attribute swap
Swaps to mace after sword/axe hit, swaps back after configurable delay.
Smart switch
Density when falling, Breach when grounded. Automatic every time.
Aim assist
Smooth target tracking while falling or always, fully configurable speed.
Trigger bot
Auto attacks when locked target hits your crosshair.
Auto stun slam
Axe stuns shields, mace slam follow-up, sword chain finish.
Pearl catch
Fires wind charge after ender pearl throw while looking up.
Lunge swap
Swaps to lunge spear on left click, auto-swaps back instantly.
Auto chestplate
Equips chestplate when close to target during fall.
